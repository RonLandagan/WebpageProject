Our Famous No-Knead Pizza Dough

Ingredients

  • 500g Bread Flour
  • 16g Salt
  • 1g Active Dry Yeast
  • 350ml water

Directions

  1. Measure and mix your flour, salt, and yeast all in one bowl. Make sure that everything is thouroughly combined.
  2. Now add your water. Use a mix the dry and wet ingredients until a shaggy dough, which should take a few minutes.
  3. Once everything is loosely held together, cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let sit at room temperature for 18-24 hours.
  4. After fermenting, scoop out your dough onto a floured surface and divide into four sections. That's four whole pizzas!
  5. Evenly coat your section with flour and stretch flat.
  6. Add your favorite sauce, cheese, and toppings and bake on the highest temperature your oven can reach for 8 minutes.
  7. Lastly, serve hot and with friends.

Our Famous Pretty Good Homemade Tomato Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 tbs Olive Oil
  • 3 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 tsp Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1 28 oz. Can of Whole Tomatoes
  • 1 tsp Onion Powder
  • 1 tsp Oregano

Directions

  1. Bring olive oil to a shimmer over medium heat, and add garlic. Cook for 1 minute or until fragrant.
  2. Add your red pepper flakes. Cook for an additional 30 seconds.
  3. Now add your can of tomatoes. Stir and break them up as much as you can.
  4. Add dried onion powder and oregano, and then simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Lastly, pour your sauce into a blender and puree until smooth.
